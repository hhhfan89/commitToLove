OUR STORY

A path to love

The couple meets in college where they attend the same course. It isn't love at first sight though: in fact she is attracted to another guy and he is still recovering from a past relationship. But between confiding to each other about their sentimental issues and attending Operating Systems and Logic Networks classes, a friendship between them forms. Watching episodes of Scrubs at the back of the classroom and chatting via MSN becomes a daily habit. They get to know each other better and soon their friendship transforms into love. They prepare numerous exams and projects together and they graduate at the same day. Their professional paths are divided but the sentimental bond that unites them become stronger.

After 7 years of being together they decide to transform the accademic path that brought them together into a path that will unite them for the rest of their lives. On April 30th they will make a dream come true, just like they did on graduation day. Only this time they will not be wearing laurel wreaths (typical of italian graduations): she will have a veil on and he will be in a lovely suit; but above all they will be celebrating with the people they care about most.

"Every love story is beautiful,
but my favorite is ours..."
